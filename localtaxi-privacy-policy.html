Privacy Policy

Local Taxi

Last updated: March 2026

Local Taxi ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and with whom it is shared when you use the Local Taxi mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address

This information is used to create and manage your account and to provide ride services.

Location Information

Local Taxi requires access to your device location in order to:

  • Display your position on the map
  • Determine your pickup location
  • Help drivers navigate to passengers

Location is accessed while the app is actively in use.

Real-Time Location Sharing

During an active ride, location data is shared between the driver and the passenger:

  • The passenger's pickup location and destination are shared with the assigned driver.

This sharing is limited to the duration of the ride and only between the two parties involved.

Ride Information

When a ride is requested or completed, we store:

  • Pickup and destination coordinates and addresses
  • Pickup notes entered by the passenger
  • Ride status and timestamps
  • The identities of the driver and passenger involved

This information is necessary to facilitate and record the ride transaction.

Driver Availability Data

For users registered as drivers, we additionally collect:

  • Availability status (available, busy, or offline)
  • Periodic heartbeat timestamps to confirm active presence while online

This data is used to determine which drivers are available to accept ride requests.

Push Notification Tokens

If you enable notifications, we store a device push token to send ride-related updates such as new ride requests, ride status changes, and service notifications.

Device and Diagnostic Information

We may collect limited technical information including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

This information is used solely to improve app reliability and performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect passengers with available drivers
  • Provide real-time ride tracking during active trips
  • Send ride status notifications
  • Store a record of completed rides
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Maintain the security of the platform

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Local Taxi uses trusted third-party services to operate the application. These providers may process limited data as necessary to deliver their functionality.

  • Cloud infrastructure and database – Used to store account, ride, and location data securely (Supabase)
  • Authentication – Used to manage secure sign-in and sessions (Supabase Auth)
  • Push notifications – Used to deliver ride alerts to your device (Firebase Cloud Messaging / Apple Push Notification Service)
  • Maps and routing – Used to display maps, calculate routes, and estimate distances and travel times (Apple MapKit)

Each provider processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active. Ride records are retained to support transaction history and service quality. If you request account deletion, your personal data will be removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored using industry-standard security practices. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may at any time:

  • Disable location access through your device Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Local Taxi
  • Disable push notifications through your device Settings → Notifications → Local Taxi
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the address below

Please note that disabling location access will prevent core features of the app from functioning.

Children's Privacy

Local Taxi is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the revised version will be posted within the app and on our website with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
